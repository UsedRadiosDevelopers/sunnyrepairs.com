REPAIR PROCESS

Repair Process includes:

  • Diagnosed by our experienced technicians (50 years of combined experience)
  • Radio TX and RX output reviewed
  • Hardware replaced as needed (Knobs, Housings, etc.)
  • Using the AeroFlex 3920 and AeroFlex 8800 Auto Tuners, each radio is aligned to factory specification levels, digital voice modulation, harmonics and protocols
  • This powerful testing tool provides immediate diagnosis of the radios entire performance characteristics and issues
  • The radio’s exterior is professionally cleaned or re-cased as needed to help return to its original condition as much as possible
