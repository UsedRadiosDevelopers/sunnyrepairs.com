ABOUT

Sunny Communications specializes in USED and REFURBISHED two way radios and infrastructure. We have been in business for over sixteen (16) years and operate in a 26,000 sq ft facility located in Lakewood, Colorado. Our extensive inventory allows us to provide support for P25 and older Legacy equipment, including infrastructure and systems that are no longer supported.

About

Sunny Communications is the leader in the refurbished two way radio communications market. We specialize in two way radio sales, repairs, rental and trade in services. We deal with a wide variety of customers, manufacturers and two way radio dealers. We endeavor to exceed their expectations with service, product quality and price.
